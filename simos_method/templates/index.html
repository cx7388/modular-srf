Modular SRF Weight Elicitation Tool

Elicit criteria importance weights with a modular Simos-Roy-Figueira workflow for flat-criteria decision problems.

Open Elicitation Tool

Modular Workflow

Configure SRF, SRF-II, Robust SRF, WAP, Imprecise SRF, Belief-degree Imprecise SRF, and HFL-SRF in one interface.

Interactive Deck-of-Cards

Arrange criteria and blank cards directly in the browser to encode rank-order and intensity preferences.

Transparent Results

Export normalized weights and review robustness-oriented outputs, including ASI and PCA visualizations when applicable.
